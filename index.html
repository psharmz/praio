Problem

Sometimes the enzyme is not the problem. Its missing environment is.

Nature typically builds enzymes to work in specific native cellular environments. When they are moved into very different settings, that support is often lost. In many cases, teams respond with enzyme engineering.

The idea is simple: enzymes are often taken out of the environments they are used to, and that missing support can become part of the problem.

Solution

Praio recreates and optimizes the environment around the enzyme.

Instead of relying on enzyme redesign alone, Praio gives enzymes a more supportive and tunable environment to work in. Our protocell platform recreates key features of the kind of local environment enzymes are used to, and then optimizes that environment for the specific reaction you want to run.

In other words, Praio helps restore a better working environment for the enzyme and tailor it to the reaction at hand.
